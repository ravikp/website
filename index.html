svn to git

2016-10-17 · 180 words · 1 minute read
scm
svn · git · subversion

Guidelines to migrate: You should always migrate the history. Remove sensitive information in case they are accidentally committed into older repositories. You should automate the migration process as well. Automated migration process should happen in an isolated environment and not on developers laptop. Original author should be preserved for every commit. There are multiple ways to solve. I recommend you to explore to tailor the process according to your needs.
